ABOUT

Jim Thorne was born in Oakland California and raised in Provo Utah. He graduated from Timpview High School in Provo Utah in 1983. After serving an LDS Mission to Chicago Illinois he returned to Utah in 1985 and attended Utah State University where he studied Landscape Architecture and Environmental Planning.


in 1990 he graduated from Utah State University with a Bachelor in Landscape Architecture and a minor in Business Administration and began to work at various jobs related to his profession in California and in Utah. After 2007 because of the need to aid other family members he worked part time or on a consultant basis.


In 2011 he was unable to continue in his original profession and accepted employment at Dominos Pizza as a delivery expert. In 2019 he enrolled at Helio Training and is hoping to have a career in the burgeoning technology industry here along the Wasatch front of Utah.
